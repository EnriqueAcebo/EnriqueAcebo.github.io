About

I am a third-year PhD student in Business Economics at the University of León (Spain). Through my research, I investigate how firms interact with other crucial agents of the innovation ecosystem to leverage new products, growth and competitive advantages.

I am focus on empirical research uding ideas from various disciplines to study innovation, including economics, geography, and business strategy. My work provides insights for managers and policymakers.

In 2018, I was awarded one of the seventeen FPU predoctoral fellowships available in Spain for doctoral studies in Business and Economics. As part of the FPU program, I teach practice lessons at "Innovation Management" and "Management Foundations" courses. During the first quarter of 2022, I have visited the Department of Strategy and Innovation of the Copenhague Business School hosted by Prof. Mecedes Delgado.

My work has been published in well-known journals like Business Strategy and the Environment, Science and Public Policy and European Journal of Innovation Management and presented in prestigious international conferences such as DRUID and ACEDE.
