Enrique Acebo
Assistant Professor of Management

About

Enrique Acebo is a graduate in Business Management and Administration and doctor Summa Cum Laude with an International Mention in Business Economics from the University of León, Burgos, Salamanca and Valladolid. He is currently an assistant professor at the University of León, where he teaches in the degrees of Business Management and Aerospace Engineering. His research focuses on strategy and innovation.

In his publications Enrique Acebo analyzes how the relationships between the agents of the innovative ecosystem increase or reduce the probability of introducing innovations, new products, business growth and competitive advantages. He belongs to the Research Group in Business Management (Grupo de investigación en Economía de la Empresa, GIDE). Other areas of research interest are environmental innovation, evaluation of public policies and operations management. Enrique has published in different high-impact magazines such as Business Strategy and the Environment, Science and Public Policy and European Journal of Innovation Management.
