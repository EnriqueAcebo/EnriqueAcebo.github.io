Enrique Acebo
Assistant Professor of Management

About

Enrique Acebo is an Assistant Professor in Management at the University of León's Department of Management and Business Economics. His research focuses on eco-innovation, the twin transition (green and digital), and the emerging new space economy. He specialises in causal inference and econometric analysis, with expertise in panel data and difference-in-differences methodologies.

Enrique has published in journals such as Industry and Innovation, Science and Public Policy, and Business Strategy and the Environment. His research explores the impact of climate change on eco-innovation, the twin transition, and the new space economy, focusing on SMEs and sustainability strategies.

He has gained international experience as a Miguel Dols Fellow at the London School of Economics and as a visiting researcher at Copenhagen Business School. Enrique collaborates on industry-focused projects, including IRPAS, addressing innovation in the aerospace sector.

Enrique has presented at leading conferences such as DRUID and ACEDE. He serves as Research Coordinator for ACEDE Operations and Technology Division and is the Social Media Editor of Industry and Innovation.

His teaching experience includes courses on operations management and innovation management. He also leads workshops on causal inference using Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs). His interests include eco-innovation, the twin transition, and the new space economy, contributing to strategies for sustainable transformation.
