Business Ecosystems and Sustainable Innovation

Ongoing

Funding: Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation

Overview

This research project explores how business ecosystems drive sustainable innovation practices, examining the interplay between different stakeholders and their collective impact on environmental outcomes.

Research Objectives

  • Understand how business ecosystem dynamics influence sustainable innovation adoption
  • Identify key stakeholder interactions that promote environmental outcomes
  • Develop frameworks for ecosystem-level sustainability strategies
  • Provide policy recommendations for fostering sustainable business ecosystems

Methodology

The project combines quantitative analysis of firm-level data with qualitative case studies of successful sustainable business ecosystems. We employ econometric techniques to identify causal relationships and network analysis to map ecosystem interactions.

Expected Outcomes

The project aims to contribute both theoretical insights and practical guidelines for managers and policymakers seeking to leverage business ecosystems for sustainable development.
