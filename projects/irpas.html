IRPAS - Inflatable Remotely Piloted Airship

Ongoing

Type: Industry Collaboration Project

Overview

IRPAS (Inflatable Remotely Piloted Airship) is an industry-focused initiative addressing innovation challenges in the aerospace sector. The project explores cutting-edge technological applications in remotely piloted airship systems, combining academic research with practical industry needs.

Project Objectives

  • Develop innovative solutions for inflatable airship technology
  • Bridge the gap between academic research and industry application
  • Explore new business models in the aerospace sector
  • Contribute to the emerging new space economy

Research Contribution

From a management perspective, this project provides insights into innovation processes in high-technology industries, technology transfer mechanisms, and the dynamics of university-industry collaboration in the aerospace sector.

Industry Partners

The project involves collaboration with aerospace industry partners, combining their technical expertise with academic research capabilities to develop practical solutions for next-generation airship systems.
